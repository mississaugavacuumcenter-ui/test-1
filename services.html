    Retractable hose systems

    Retractable hose systems: cleaner storage and easier everyday use

    A retractable central vacuum system stores the hose inside the wall tubing instead of in a closet or utility room. When it is time to clean, you pull out only the hose length you need, lock it in place, and begin cleaning. When you finish, the suction from the power unit retracts the hose back into the wall. Official Hide-A-Hose guidance notes that hoses can be available up to 60 feet and that installation may be possible in both new construction and some existing homes, depending on access and layout.

    Easier storage

    The hose stays hidden inside the wall system, helping reduce clutter and making the central vacuum easier to manage day to day.

    Faster setup and cleanup

    Pull out the hose, clean the area you need, and let the system retract it when the job is done.

    Cleaner everyday experience

    You only use the hose length required for the space, which can make routine cleaning feel more organized and efficient.

    Excellent for new builds and selective retrofits

    It is ideal for new homes and may also be possible in some existing homes where pipe routing and access allow it.
