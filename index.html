Retractable hose systems

A cleaner, easier way to use central vacuum

Retractable systems store the hose inside the wall and pull it back in when you are done, making daily cleaning more convenient and easier to store. Official system sources describe in-wall storage, suction-powered retraction, and hose lengths up to 60 feet.
